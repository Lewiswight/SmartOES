Wide-Area Connectivity

The Digi gateway looks for a transport path to the next stop in the application flow, typically over a wide-area-network (WAN). WANs may be private or public, IP-based or other, terrestrial or wireless (cellular). In this demonstration, the WAN bridges the Digi gateway (ConnectPort X4) and the Digi solution enablement platforms (Connectware® Messaging & Connectware® Storage).
